Question: Describe Cottage 11

Answer: Cottage 11 is a cozy two-storey cottage, carefully designed for comfort, warmth, and family-friendly living. The ground floor, accessible directly from the car parking level, includes a furnished lounge with beautiful scenic views, a luxurious master bedroom with an en-suite bathroom, and a modern kitchen with a dining area. A key highlight of Cottage 11 is its beautiful attic space located above the master bedroom, which serves as an additional cozy sleeping area. This feature is especially popular with families and children, adding both charm and extra functionality to the cottage. The private balcony offers relaxing views and a peaceful natural atmosphere. The lower level hosts two well-designed bedrooms sharing a common bathroom, along with a fully equipped kitchen, making Cottage 11 an excellent choice for comfortable family stays.
